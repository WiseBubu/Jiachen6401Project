Total Healthcare Spendings in 2013-2018

As we can see in the tree map, United States has the highest healthcare expenditure total in all countries. In the second layer, healthcare expenditure is growing from 2013 to 2018 in the United States. China is the second country spending the most healthcare, within 6 years, China spent more than 200 billion dollars in healthcare.




Healthcare vs GDP (Per Capita) in 2013-2018

United States has the highest per capita healthcare and per capita GDP. In 2013, Australia had highest per capita GDP, but it went down later. For the most countries, health per capita value goes up along with GDP per capita growing. In other words, healthcare per capita correlated to per capita GDP positively for the most countries.



Fastest Growing Country in Healthcare Expenditure

From the above graph, China, South Korea and Saudi Arabia have highest growing rates in healthcare expenditure. Compared with 2013, China spent 255 billion dollars more in 2018.
