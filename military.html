Total Military Spendings in 2013-2018

As we expected, United States spent the most amount of money on military expenditure. But from 2013 to 2018, the military spending of the United States did not grow too much. China is the second country spent most military expenditure, it has increased at least $60 billion in 6 years. Canada has the least military Spendings in all countries.




Military vs GDP (Per Capita) in 2013-2018

Saudi Arabia's military expense per capita kept growing, but it's GDP per capita did not grow. The United States's military per capita dropped while it's GDP per capita grew.
