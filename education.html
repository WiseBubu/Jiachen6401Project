Total Education Spendings in 2013-2018

The United States spent the most amount of money on education from 2013-2018 among all countries. Within six years, the education expenditure went up more than 100 billion dollars in the United States.




Education vs GDP (Per Capita) in 2013-2018

Australia had highest education and GDP per capita in 2013, then these two values dramatically dropped to $1692 in 2018. For the rest of the countries, we can see that the education per capita grows when GDP per capita rises. Thses two values are correlated to each other positively for most of countries here. In above graph, the size of the circle means the population of the country.



Fastest Growing Country in Educational Expenditure

India, China, South Korea and the United States have spent more money on educational since 2013. Compared to China' growing amount of 78 billion dollars, India only spent 26 more billion dollars more. But India has the highest growth rate of the rest of countries, which is 46%.
